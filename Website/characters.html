There are 76 characters in Super Smash Bros currently, but we are still awaiting future fighters that have yet to be announced. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate has one of the largest rosters of any fighting game ever made, bringing back more than 60 returning characters, and adding more than a dozen fresh new faces. Right out of the box there are 76 playable characters from across video game history, plus more downloadable characters that have joined the game since release. Super Smash Bros Ultimate is the largest game of the franchise so far, including every single character from each iteration of the game. Series included are very popular such as: Super Mario bros, Pokemon, Fire Emblem, Metroid, Kirby and dozens more. The "Super Smash Bros. Ultimate" fighters pack includes another five downloadable characters for $25. So far the pack includes Joker from "Persona 5," Banjo-Kazooie, The Hero from "Dragon Quest," and Terry Bogard from "Fatal Fury" and "The King of Fighters." The fifth character hasn't been revealed, but Nintendo plans to release the last character by February 2020.

The strongest characters of Super Smash Bros Ultimate

Inevitably, there will be some characters that are evidently stronger than others throughout the roster. Whether you play the game competitively or casually, you will have noticed that some characters are far stronger than others. That isn't to say that the weaker cast should be dismissed, as they still have a few tricks up their sleeves.
