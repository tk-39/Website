About us

Discover what we are about and what our goal is

Our Mission

At The Super Smash Brothers, we want to build a bigger and better community for Super Smash bros. We would like to celebrate the release of the biggest crossover ever in the franchise by creating this website and helping the community get better at the game and keeping them up to date on the latest news. We are a non-profit organisation who only seeks to benefit the community by keeping this website running and keeping it up to date.

Please consider subscribing to our news letter to receive recent updates and access to our weekly newsletter, featuring the latest tech and news on future DLC fighters.

What we do

We are a small team of gamers looking to express our love for the Super Smash bros franchise, particularly Super Smash bros Ultimate. We want to share with you, the playerbase what you can do to improve your skills and keeping you up to date on the latest smash news. You can expect consistent updates from us so make sure to come back to our site and stay informed. On our website you can find a handy guide to improve your gameplay, a character list including a tier list and a news page, where you can find the latest smash news.
